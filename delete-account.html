QuranPal — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This page explains how users of QuranPal (the app listed on Google Play) can request deletion of their account and associated personal data stored by us.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to request deletion:

shakeelkhalid786@gmail.com

Use the subject line: QuranPal Account Deletion Request

Steps to delete your QuranPal account

  1. Email us at shakeelkhalid786@gmail.com from the email address linked to your QuranPal account (or tell us which sign-in method you used: Google, Apple, or email/password).
  2. Include in your message:
    • The email address or Apple/Google account used to sign in
    • A clear statement that you want your QuranPal account and cloud data deleted
  3. We will verify that the request comes from the account owner. We may reply with a short confirmation email before processing.
  4. After deletion is complete, we will email you to confirm. Your Firebase login for QuranPal will no longer work.
  5. Optional — remove local data on your device: uninstall the QuranPal app or clear app storage in your phone settings. Local preferences and cached data on your device are not removed automatically when we delete your cloud account.

Processing time

We aim to complete account and data deletion within 7 business days after we verify your request. In rare cases, processing may take up to 30 days.

What data is deleted

When your QuranPal account is deleted, we delete the following data tied to your account:

Data type Description
Account credentials Your Firebase Authentication record (email, display name, unique user ID, and sign-in provider link)
Reading progress Main Quran goal and side reading progress synced to the cloud
Bookmarks Saved verses and bookmark folders
Liked verses Verse likes stored under your account
Prayer tracking Prayer completion logs synced to the cloud
Supporter / tip status In-app purchase supporter summary linked to your user ID (iap_subscriptions)

What data may be kept

Data type Why it may be kept Retention period
Purchase transaction records Google Play and App Store require records for refunds, fraud prevention, and tax/accounting. After account deletion, purchase records may be retained without your personal account identifier. Up to 7 years, or as required by applicable law
Server and security logs Short-term technical logs used to protect the service and investigate abuse Typically up to 90 days
Data on your device Theme, reciter, cached prayer times, and other local settings stay on your phone until you uninstall the app or clear app data Until you remove the app or clear storage

Using QuranPal without an account

You can use many QuranPal features without signing in. If you never created an account, there is no cloud account to delete — uninstalling the app removes local data from your device.
