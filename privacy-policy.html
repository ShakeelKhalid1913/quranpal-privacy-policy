QuranPal Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to QuranPal ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data that we must collect. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with a seamless Quran reading and listening experience:

  • User Preferences: We store your app preferences locally on your device, including:
    • Theme preferences (Light/Dark mode)
    • Reading settings
    • Selected language
    • Audio playback settings
  • Firebase Services: We use Firebase for app functionality, which may collect:
    • Basic usage statistics
    • App performance data
    • Crash reports

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing core app functionality
  • Saving your preferences locally
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Enhancing user experience

Information Storage

We store your preferences locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not:

  • Store personal information on external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your information for marketing purposes

Internet Access

QuranPal requires internet access for:

  • Streaming Quran recitations
  • Firebase services functionality
  • App performance monitoring

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. Your preferences are securely stored on your device and are not accessible to other applications.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored preferences
  • Delete your local data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of Firebase analytics
  • Request information about your data

Open Source Attribution

QuranPal uses open-source Quran texts and recitations that are freely available for public use. We respect and adhere to the terms of use of all third-party content.
